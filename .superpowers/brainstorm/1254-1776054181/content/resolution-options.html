Minimal Set

Just the essentials. Faster to build and test, expand later if players request more.

1280x720 (720p) 1920x1080 (1080p) 1170x2532 (iPhone) 2048x2732 (iPad Pro)
How this works: The game renders at a base resolution (1280x720) and scales up cleanly to larger sizes. iOS resolutions are portrait — the game will auto-rotate the layout for mobile. During development we build at 720p and everything scales from there.
