Act I · Ch. 1
A Mysterious Egg

While tidying up the shop after a long afternoon, you hear a faint sound — a soft, rhythmic tapping from beneath the counter.

"Something stirs within that egg. It has chosen you, Keeper."

Elder Mira appears in the doorway, her eyes gleaming with recognition. She sets a worn leather satchel on the counter and gestures toward the glowing egg nestled inside.
